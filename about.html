Welcome to NMAMIT Campus

NMAM Institute of Technology is located in the beautiful campus of Paneer, Deralakatte, Mangalore. We provide transportation facilities for students and are well-connected by public transport. You'll find ATMs and cafeterias on our campus. We offer separate hostels for men and women with all necessary facilities.

We prioritize a safe and friendly environment for all students and have a strong anti-ragging committee in place to address any concerns promptly.

Vision

To build a humane society through excellence in education and health care

Mission

To develop NITTE as a Centre of Excellence imparting quality education, generating competent, skilled manpower to face the scientific and social challenges, with a high degree of credibility, integrity, ethical standards, and social concern

Our Values

At NMAMIT, we are committed to fostering a culture of learning, innovation, and social responsibility. Our core values include:

  • Excellence in Education
  • Integrity and Ethics
  • Community Engagement
  • Diversity and Inclusion
  • Environmental Sustainability

We believe in nurturing the holistic development of our students and preparing them to be leaders in their chosen fields.
